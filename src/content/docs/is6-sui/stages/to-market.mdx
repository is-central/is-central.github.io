<StrategySection title="Stage Analysis" author="Danger_Asian" defaultOpen={true}>
This stage features enemies from the Operation Lucent Arrowhead event. Ranged enemies can target allied as long as they are within "line of sight", the enemy can attack as long as the enemy is along the same straight line as your units. 

**NM and EM differences**\
EM is a HUGE step up from NM with two Light Rapid Crossbowman substituted with Heavy Crossbowman Ambusher, stunning any units hit by their ranged attacks as well as being invisible while not attacking. A statuegeist is also present on the map, applying attack speed, movement speed increase and damage reduction. 

## Notable Enemies
* Heavy Crossbowman Ambusher: Deals moderate arts damage, stuns and is mostly invisible
* Armored Scout: Applies fragile to allied units attacking him, making your units take more damage. 
* Light Rapid Crossbowman: Normal ranged enemy units with line of sight mechanic.
* Statuegeist: Spawns Light Rapid Crossbowman at regular intervals. 

## Stage Variants / Permutations:
Random positioning exists for the crossbowman (light and heavy variants). Adapt your positioning accordingly

## Notable Sui hours and Risk Coin debuffs
West (enemy Max HP) and South (enemy Def up) make enemies beefier. 




</StrategySection>

## Stage Strategies

<StrategySection title="Raidian starter squad" author="Danger Asian">

On NM, Raidian S2 is a reasonable choice for faster clear times

On EM, Raidian S1 is pivotal especially if one wants to kill the statuegeist and is willing to stall for it. 

Stack your summons preemptively away from line of sight of the Heavy Crossbowman Ambushers. Preemptively kill said crossbowman if your dps permits.

Turn off the statuegeist midway through the stage so your block does not get overwhelmed.

Keep the Armored Scout alive so they can be stalled. 

Reposition your damage operators to destroy the statuegeist as well as to kill the Light Rapid Crossbowman when they start spawning again due to the statuegeist being reactivated.
