<StrategySection title="Stage Analysis" author="Danger_Asian" defaultOpen={true}>
This stage features mechanics from the Twilight of Wolumonde Event. 

A Gramaphone is inactive on this map. Placing allied units adjacent to the Gramaphone charges it up. When fully charged, attacks deal Ranged Arts damage equal to a percentage of the enemy's Max HP and periodically heals allied units. 

**NM and EM differences**\
1 Bottle Swordsman is replaced with Mudrock Colossus
## Notable Enemies
* Leithanian Whisperer: Long ranged Arts caster that is immune to stun, silence and freeze. Will try to contest Gramaphones.
* Bottle Swordsman: Ranged appartition enemy with small attack range. Attacks can apply talismans on allied units. Somewhat beefy and needs to be dealt early.
* Mudrock Colossus: Melee enemy that only appears on the EM variant. Extremely high HP but folds under an ally-controlled Gramaphone. 
## Stage Variants / Permutations:
No particular noteworthy stage permutations aside from enemy positioning order
## Notable Sui hours and Risk Coin debuffs
Hsu Hui can make openers slightly tricky, but not significant trouble should exist.
