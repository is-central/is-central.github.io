<StrategySection title="Stage Analysis" author="Danger_Asian" defaultOpen={true}>
This stage features enemies in Operation Originium Dust Event. These enemies summon support drones from the Mobile Tactical Hangar when fulfilling certain conditions. 

Roadblocks exist to change enemy pathing when destroyed.

Ursus Civilians spawn from the top left Incursion Point and cost 1 LP per death. 
**NM and EM differences**\
EM adds a statuegeist which spawns Infected Patrol Captains, which can hurt Ursus Civilians. 
Two extra roadblocks appear. 
## Notable Enemies
* Infected Patrol Captain: Standard enemy foot soldier that can attack Ursus Civilians.
* Ursus Civilain: Important protection targets or else cost LP when dead.  
* Tactical Crossbowman: Summons Scrap drones locking on to the first allied unit in their attack range. 
* Scrap: Enemy drone with low targetting priority and bombards allied units, dealing splash physical damage and lowering allied defence. Significant threat and needs to be baited. 
* War Phantom: Summons Scrap targetting the first allied unit hit. Spawns Anvil when at 50% HP.
* Anvil: Enemy drone that heals enemy units.

## Stage Variants / Permutations:
No known permutations exist. 

## Notable Sui hours and Risk Coin debuffs
Hsu Hui (Talisman on operators upon first deploy) can affect openers, especially when attempting to neutralize the statuegeist.

## Stage Strategies

<StrategySection title="Raidian starter squad" author="Danger Asian">

Open with Raidian running S2 on the bottom right ranged tile.

Breaking the roadblocks lets the Ursus Civilians route towards the top lane away from the Infected Patrol Captains.

Bait/kill the initial Tactical Crossbowman at range to free up ranged tiles in the center of the map. 

Destroy the statuegeist as soon as possible.

Dish damage at the War Phantoms when they stand at the top left checkpoint. 

Bait the subsequent Tactical Crossbowman with your units.
