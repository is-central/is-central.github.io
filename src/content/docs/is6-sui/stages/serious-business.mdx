<StrategySection title="Stage Analysis" author="Danger_Asian" defaultOpen={true}>
This stage mainly consists of slugs, occasional Sarkaz Greatswordsman and Big Bob. Big Bob takes a longer route by breaking every block and standing for a while before moving on to the next block. 

**NM and EM differences**\
EM has reasonably high stats, especially for Big Bob. +50% Max HP on all enemies and an additional 20% on Big Bob pushes his effective HP to 95k on D15. 

Big Bob also has reduced waiting time on his pathing, resulting in him taking less time to reach the objective point. 
## Notable Enemies
* Big Bob: High HP and reasonably high defence (960). Is a -1 LP leak. 

## Stage Variants / Permutations:
Statuegeist on this map randomly appears in one of the three cubicles. 
## Notable Sui hours and Risk Coin debuffs
West (enemy Max HP) and South (enemy Def up) make enemies, especially Big Bob, beefier. 




</StrategySection>

## Stage Strategies

<StrategySection title="Raidian starter squad" author="Danger Asian">

Raidian S2 is the way to go, dedicate one summon to destroying the statuegeist by placing the summon adjacent to the statuegeist. 

With Wood Feeds Flames coin active (-30% enemy Max HP), killing Big Bob on EM could be possible but is just a -1 leak in a worst case scenario.
