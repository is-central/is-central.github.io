<StrategySection title="Stage Analysis" author="Danger_Asian" defaultOpen={true}>
This stage features standard enemies in Sui's garden. Enemies funnel along a single path to the objective point. An Ink Brew spawns late during the stage to provide enemy buffs. Shadow scholars wait on the top row initially and move at the last wave. 

**NM and EM differences**\
EM is a HUGE step up from NM with a statuegeist along the main path that all enemies pass through
## Notable Enemies
* Ink Brew: Ranged physical enemy that has a similar effect as a statuegeist, applying ASPD, movement speed and 50% Phys/Arts damage reduction.
* Talismans: Appear top right from the aerial incursion point. Seals operator skills and deals damage. 
* Shadow Scholar: Standard Sui's Garden enemy unit with moderately high HP pools. Consumes a talisman to restore HP when HP goes below 40%. 
* Statuegeist: Spawns Biteys at regular intervals.

## Stage Variants / Permutations:
No known permutations exist. 

## Notable Sui hours and Risk Coin debuffs
West (enemy Max HP) make enemies beefier.

## Stage Strategies

<StrategySection title="Raidian starter squad" author="Danger Asian">

On NM, Raidian S2 is a reasonable choice for faster clear times. Preemptively clear the top row to minimize pressure on the last wave. 

On EM, Raidian S1 is a safer combination 
S2 is also possible with other damage operators. The general idea is to camp the top left to not allow the enemies to get buffed by the statuegeist. 

The statuegeist should be silenced early. A ranged operator should be placed adjacent to the statuegeist to destroy it. 

The clear is left as an exercise for the reader.
