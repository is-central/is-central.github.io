<StrategySection title="Stage Analysis" author="Danger_Asian" defaultOpen={true}>
This stage features enemies from the Invitation to Wine event. These enemies spawn hit-count based appartitions (also known as furniture), which have low lifepoints but all damage taken is reduced to 1.

Furniture enemies spawn from the top left Incursion Point. The top right one spawns Shadow Scholars. The lone Incursion point at the bottom left spawns Mirror Tianshis.

**NM and EM differences**\
EM adds an extra statuegeist, which spawns talismans

Additional 1 Mirror Tianshi appears at the start


## Notable Enemies
* Grindstone: Hit-count enemy that spawns Wooden Seals upon death
* Mirror Tianshi: Ranged enemy that does not normally attack. Instead it periodically locks onto an ally on the field (infinite range) and sends a talisman towards them at fast speed. The talisman can be intercepted.
* Statuegeist: Spawns Talismans

## Stage Variants / Permutations:
Only one stage permutation exists.

Watch out for Hsu Hui openers

## Stage Strategies

<StrategySection title="Raidian starter squad" author="Danger Asian">

Hold your defence line slightly forward so that you have enough room to kill the Wooden Seals. Raidian S2 is decent at this due to her summons performing multi-hit attacks when skill is active. 

The Tianshi Mirror can be squeezed down the hole with a melee ally provided fear relics like Charcoal Mask are not obtained. Place the ally directly on top of the Tianshi Mirror when it is stationary. 

On EM this can be performed during the opener to squeeze the first Tianshi Mirror down the hole.
</StrategySection>
