<StrategySection title="Stage Analysis" author="Danger_Asian" defaultOpen={true}>
This stage features a continuous line of enemies parading to the objective. A shieldguard is present with reasonably high HP and Def. As long as the shieldguard is alive, laneholding should be easy. 

**NM and EM differences**\
Besides the standard increase in Atk and HP, Bloodboil Knightclub Elties gain extra 30% Atk
## Notable Enemies
* Bloodboil Knightclub Elties: Gains Atk and ASPD when an enemy is killed. 
* Tiacauh Fanatic: Brawler crocodile which reduces ally defence permanently per hit. 
## Stage Variants / Permutations:
No particular noteworthy stage permutations aside from enemy positioning order
## Notable Sui hours and Risk Coin debuffs
No known Risk coin debuffs causing issues
