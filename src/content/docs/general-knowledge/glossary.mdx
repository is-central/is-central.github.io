## Other
<div style={{ display: "flex", justifyContent: "center" }}>
| Term | Description |
|:----:|:-----------:|
| External modifier | Modifiers applied before a stage begins, these cannot change within the stage and are displayed on the operator stats before deployment. |
| Internal modifier | Modifiers applied after a stage beings, these can usually change within the stage and are reflected on the operator stats when its activation condition is fulfilled. |
</div>
