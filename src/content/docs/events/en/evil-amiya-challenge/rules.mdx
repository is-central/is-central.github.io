## Evil Amiya Challenge
It's time for IS5 to get its tournament! There will be no scoring, no leaderboard, no competition with other players. There is only one objective. Survive!

## Challenge
Complete endings 2, 4 and 5 at difficulty 18 while having the relic Reality of the End. For those of you who are newer to the mode (30 or less Arch Glyph’s used), the great Demon King Amiya will allow you to complete the challenge with Framework of the End!

## Rules
* You can only recruit 2 of the following operators: Mon3tr, Tragodia, Wisadel
* 200 ingot withdraw limit

Anyone who completes the challenge will be scored with 1 point in the final scoreboard, and will receive a **Monthly Card!**

Participants will register for a time slot and record* and upload their run. You only get one chance so come prepared!

*those who are unable to record can dm the organizers

### Server
* You may play on an EN, JP, TC or KR account.
* You may not play on a SC server account.

### Reset/disconnect
* You may not reset the run for any reason.
* If you disconnect or quit the run for any reason, your run will be counted as a loss.

### Opener
* You may prepare 2 Plans, Hot Water Kettle, 2 Hope or Ingots as your floor 3 buff.
* You may not choose either random Collectible buffs.
* You may not open with As Your Heart Desires (random vouchers)
* You may not open with Mimic Squad (random squad effects)

## New Player Rules
* If you are a newer player (30 or less Arch Glyph’s used), you may register to do the easier challenge which is endings 2, 4 and 5 with the relic Framework of the End.
* To do this, submit a screenshot of your account like the one below (IS5 Lobby -> Soul Communication Code (top of screen) -> Data Rollback -> Personal Stats and then scroll down).
* In your recording of your run, you must also show your personal stats and show your number of Arch Glyphs

## Recording
* Your recording must begin on the Arknights homescreen to verify the server time, the time must match within 180s of your scheduled time (discretion up to organisers).
* You then navigate to the IS5 tab and begin your run, you may have already prepared a run with Plans, Kettle, Hope, or Ingots as your floor 3 buff. You are not allowed to begin at floor 1.
* When your run finishes, show your operators, Collectibles, and Thoughts in the endscreen, then click the endscreen ONCE to display your score.
* When your score finishes displaying, return to the Arknights homescreen to verify the run time.
* You must record continuously from start to end to the best of your ability
* You must record the run at a watchable quality and framerate to the best of your ability. If the video quality is too poor, we may be unable to score your run accurately.

Here is an <a href="https://youtu.be/ITvxIA3hY1Q" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">example run</a> following the tournament rules and recording specifications for a match scheduled for June 3rd at 21:00 UTC, this would be equivalent to 14:00 UTC -7 which is the time zone the game's server uses. In the schedule page you can check the time every run starts at any time zone to make sure it matches the time in the homescreen.
