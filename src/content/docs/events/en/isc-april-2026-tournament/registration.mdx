## Tournament information
The IS-C April 2026 tournament consists of each participant completing three IS6 Ending 4 runs. Only the highest-scoring run among the three will be counted toward the final result.

Additional details about the tournament are available on the <a href="/events/en/isc-april-2026-tournament/rules/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">rules page</a>.

## Registration
Registration does not have any requirements and is done through a <a href="https://forms.gle/5R5VMkT7aP3yTqZ8A" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Google registration form</a>.

The tournament will be organized through our <a href="https://discord.gg/fqRBzQWVfK" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Discord server</a>, which will be a requirement to join in order to participate.

### Categories
Participants must select one of the two available categories:
* IS6 d10 ED4
* IS6 d15 ED2 + ED4

### Scheduling
* Participants must schedule when they will complete their three runs. Each run must be recorded in accordance with the <a href="/events/en/isc-april-2026-tournament/rules/#recording" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">recording specifications</a>.
* Scheduling is flexible and may be arranged at the participant's convenience within the tournament timeframe.
* You must enter your starting time (in UTC-7, AK EN server time) of your run(s) in the sign-up form in the #iscmt-sign-up Discord channel.
* You must sign up BEFORE you play.
* If you cannot play your run at your scheduled time for exceptional reasons (not “I slept in”), ping @karpechan, @larfa, and @kaltheresa to reschedule, preferably before the scheduled time passes.
* The tournament begins at 17:00 15th of April and ends at 17:00 15th of May (UTC -7)

We are strict about scheduling to ensure no one unfairly plays more runs.
