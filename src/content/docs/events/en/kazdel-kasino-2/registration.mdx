## Registration Requirements
- Players 1 and 2 must provide screenshots of clearing **Facing souls 15+** with the **Endless key** relic.
- Player 3 must provide screenshots of clearing **Facing souls 18** with either the **Endless key** or **Anasa's Karma relic** without the **Prophet Horn relic**.
- Players must be able to stream runs on Discord.
- Runs may be streamed on <a href="https://twitch.tv/oyana1234" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">twitch.tv/oyana1234</a>.
- Players must be members of the <a href="https://discord.gg/fqRBzQWVfK" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">IS Central Discord</a>.
